# Apex Human Company thesis

## In one sentence

Apex makes garments built to be *worn*, not displayed — at 220 GSM
and Naval Forces standards, with two business modes: bulk orders
for institutions, and Apex Experimental for crowd-sourced one-offs.

## The two business modes

1. **Apex (bulk)**
   Corporate / institutional bulk orders. Existing client: Indian
   Naval Forces. Predictable revenue, used to fund capacity expansion.

2. **Apex Experimental**
   Crowd-sourced custom orders. Users post video of a clothing problem
   (loose collar, cracked print, missing design) and tag Apex; Apex
   produces the fixed/customized piece and ships it. Each item is a
   one-off. Drives viral marketing + cash flow + product feedback loop.

## The 220 GSM thesis

Most apparel optimizes for cost. Apex optimizes for *honesty of
material*. 220 GSM means heavier fabric, longer wear, harder to fake
quality. The number is on the label deliberately — it's a public
commitment.

## Why it works together

- Apex Experimental → viral reach → demand
- Demand → revenue → facility expansion
- Facility expansion → ability to serve Naval Forces and other
  bulk clients reliably
- Naval Forces revenue → stability that keeps Experimental
  experimental

## Forcing functions

- **6-month target**: go viral with Apex Experimental; expand
  facility to fulfill all promised custom orders; ₹1Cr business

## What Apex is not

- Not a fashion brand
- Not "premium" in the trend sense
- Not D2C in the Insta-aesthetic sense
- Not a 200 GSM brand
