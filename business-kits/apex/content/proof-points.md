## Two arms (capabilities)

### Regular Apex (bulk B2B)
Corporate uniforms / merchandise / gifting. Existing business.
Factory-direct pricing eliminates the 4–8× markup that brand-name
manufacturers charge.

### Apex Experimental (virality + growth engine — not yet on
public site as of 2026-05)
- **Mechanic**: user tags Apex on Instagram/social with a video of
  a clothing problem (loose collar, cracked print, missing design
  on plain tee, etc.)
- Apex produces the fixed / customized product and ships it
- Each piece is a one-off custom order
- Two purposes simultaneously: (1) viral marketing — every
  fulfillment is a shareable content piece; (2) cash-flow pump —
  individual custom orders fund facility expansion for bulk B2B

## Competitive positioning

### vs retail brand merchandise (Ralph Lauren, Lacoste, US Polo)
Same fabric. Same yarn. Same construction. The only difference is
the label sewn into the neck and the price. Apex sells the
underlying product without the brand tax.

### vs middleman aggregators / corporate gifting platforms
Zero middleman — Apex is the supply chain. The aggregator's
commission goes into client value instead of into a margin.

### vs other Indian manufacturers (pure CMT / cut-make-trim shops)
Design-led. In-house studio. AQL 2.5 QC. Naval Forces-grade
delivery standards. Most Indian manufacturers are pure-CMT;
Apex is end-to-end including design, branding, and program
management.
