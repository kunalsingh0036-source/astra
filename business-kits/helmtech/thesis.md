## The thesis in one sentence

HelmTech is **India's Intelligence Layer** — the foundational models,
MCP protocol fabric, and execution products that let AI *do* things,
not just talk. The website-and-agent arm at helmtech.in is the
revenue engine; the platform thesis is the institutional bet.

## Two positions, one company

HelmTech runs two surfaces simultaneously. Both are legitimate; the
audience persona picks which one to lead with.

### Surface A — SMB-customer (helmtech.in)
"We build growth engines — not just websites." Web design + AI agents
for Indian SMBs at ₹15k–₹45k. This is the lived revenue line:
₹1Cr-from-this-arm by Oct 2026 is the forcing function.

The pitch is concrete: a doctor gets 40% more appointments after
the website + agent goes live; a restaurant saves Zomato/Swiggy
commissions by taking orders directly; a CA stops repeating
intake-form questions in WhatsApp. *Outcomes, not technology.*

### Surface B — Investor / institutional (the deck)
"India's Intelligence Layer." A national-scale infrastructure thesis:
the rails models plug into, the protocol fabric for India, the
execution layer for the intelligence economy. This is the $20–30M
Series A bet, the IndiaAI Mission frame, the sovereign-AI argument.

The two are connected: the SMB-agent arm is **product velocity at
consumer pricing** — proof that HelmTech can ship execution-grade
AI at scale. Every SMB customer is a node on the rails. The arm
isn't a side hustle; it's the thesis at human scale.

## The three layers (institutional thesis)

1. **Foundational models layer** — sovereign AI capacity. India needs
   its own LLMs trained on Indian data, with Indian governance, in
   Indian languages. Sarvam, Krutrim, BharatGen are early entrants.
   HelmTech's bet is that foundational models are the commodity
   layer eventually, not the moat.

2. **MCP layer** — the standardized protocol fabric that lets models
   *act*. Tool-use, agent orchestration, cross-system execution.
   This is the wedge — the lasting differentiation. Every model
   eventually plugs into MCP; whoever builds the dominant MCP fabric
   for India owns the rails.

3. **Execution products layer** — end-user AI products that
   demonstrate what the rails enable. **Shotgun** (the
   consumer/prosumer AI platform — 520+ tools, 15+ integrations) and
   **Buckshot** (AI film-making, aggregating Veo/Sora/Kling/Runway/
   Higgsfield/Pika) are the flagship products. **Astra** (Kunal's
   personal agent OS) is effectively Shotgun v0 — the productized
   version of what runs Kunal himself. The website-and-agent arm at
   helmtech.in is the SMB-distribution face of this layer.

## The wedge

Three things together — none in isolation:

1. **India-native** — built for Indian data, languages, regulation,
   and use cases. Not a port of Anthropic / OpenAI for India.
2. **Execution-first** — AI that acts in the real world (sends
   emails, books appointments, files compliance, runs ads). Not a
   chat toy.
3. **MCP-standardized** — open protocol fabric. Models, tools, and
   agents from anywhere can plug in. The opposite of a walled
   garden.

## Why now

- India's AI market is consolidating around foundational models;
  the *application* layer is the next wave and is wide open.
- MeitY's IT Rules 2026 (effective Feb 20) impose AI labeling
  requirements — favors platforms with clear provenance and
  governance built in. HelmTech has compliance as a default, not
  retrofit.
- The IndiaAI Mission has explicit funding for deep-tech AI
  startups. NVIDIA + Peak XV + Elevation + Z47 are co-investing
  in this category right now.
- LLM costs have collapsed enough that consumer-priced AI products
  are economically viable. Shotgun's $20–50/month tier is in the
  sweet spot. SMB website + agent at ₹45k all-in is profitable.

## Why not the alternatives

### Investor-side comparisons
- **vs Sarvam / Krutrim / BharatGen**: They're building the
  commodity layer (foundational models). HelmTech is building the
  layer above — the rails models plug into.
- **vs Claude / ChatGPT / Perplexity**: Foreign-built; subject to
  US data jurisdiction; not India-native. Indian data on Indian
  rails is a real procurement lever for enterprise/government.
- **vs custom enterprise solutions** (TCS, Infosys, Wipro AI arms):
  Slow, services-heavy, project-not-product. HelmTech is product
  velocity at consumer pricing.

### SMB-side comparisons
- **vs WordPress agencies / template shops**: They build static
  brochure sites. HelmTech ships growth engines that capture and
  qualify leads automatically.
- **vs hire-a-freelancer**: No accountability, no follow-through,
  no agent. HelmTech is a 1-week ship with the agent baked in.
- **vs DIY builders (Wix, Shopify)**: SMB owners don't have time
  to learn the tool. HelmTech ships it for them with strategy
  embedded.

## What we're not

To keep the thesis sharp, what HelmTech is *not*:

- Not a foundational model lab (we use them, we don't train them
  from scratch — though we may fine-tune)
- Not an AI consultancy
- Not "ChatGPT for India" — that framing concedes the model is the
  product. The MCP fabric is the product.
- Not a vertical SaaS (Shotgun is horizontal — agents for any work)
- Not a web-design agency that bolted "AI" onto its name. The agent
  is core; the website is the surface the agent lives on.
