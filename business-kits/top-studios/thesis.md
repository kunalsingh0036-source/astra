# Top Studios thesis

## In one sentence

Top Studios is the AI-native film and creative studio behind Kunal's
portfolio — internal marketing engine + external paying clients,
combining AI generation tools (Buckshot, etc.) with classical
film-making craft.

## What Top Studios does

1. **Internal marketing engine** — produces all marketing video,
   stills, and creative for HelmTech / Apex / BAY. Dogfoods Buckshot
   in production.
2. **External clients** — paid creative work for outside companies
   (brand films, ad campaigns, social content, brand kits).
3. **Brand kit creation** — productized brand guideline development
   for clients. (Powered by Astra's `draft_brand_kit` tool.)

## The AI + classical thesis

Many shops are pure-AI (text-to-video pipelines with no eye for
shot structure). Many are pure-classical (great craft, slow speed).
Top Studios sits between:

- **AI for speed** — iteration in hours, not weeks. Comp 30 versions
  in the time a traditional team comps 1.
- **Classical craft for quality** — shot lists, narrative structure,
  pacing, color grade, actual editor in the loop. AI generates the
  raw material; human craft assembles it.

## Forcing functions

- **6-month target**: external client revenue covers Top Studios'
  entire cost (including the marketing it does for HelmTech / Apex
  / BAY internally). Self-funding.
- **Current team**: 1 editor hired
- **Next hire**: AI filmmaker (someone fluent in shot lists +
  Kling/Runway/Veo prompting)

## What Top Studios is not

- Not a creative *agency* — agencies coordinate; studios produce.
- Not a generic "content creation" service.
- Not an AI consultancy.

## The internal-external duality

Top Studios marketing for HelmTech / Apex / BAY is *case-study
material* for external pitches. The portfolio writes itself:
"We made BAY's launch deck. We made Apex's brand kit. We made the
HelmTech pre-seed video." External buyers see what the studio does
*because they can see what the founder's own companies look like.*
