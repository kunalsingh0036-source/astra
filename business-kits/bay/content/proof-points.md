## The 4-vertical plan

### Vertical I — The Excellence Centre (Flagship, Mumbai)
5-story vertical village. Benchmark: UFC Performance Institute ×
Reserve Padel.

- **Ground floor — The Social Zone**: 2× outdoor glass courts
  ("Squash Porch") as live billboards + Nutrition Café. Public,
  funnel top.
- **Level 1 — The Arena**: 4× glass-back pro courts + 1× all-glass
  "Gold" show court (Olympic Squad only; CSR-funded).
- **Level 2 — The Forge**: Elite S&C — VBT tech, Keiser machines,
  plyo zones. By-application or high-ticket membership.
- **Level 3 — The Lab**: VO2 Max, Force Plate, Motion Capture,
  physio suites + orthopedic partnerships (Kokilaben/Hiranandani
  in conversation).
- **Level 4 — The Sanctuary**: Cryotherapy, contrast baths,
  infrared saunas, hyperbaric O2, pneumatic compression.

### Vertical II — BAY Experience Centres
"Wellness Paradise" framing — Squash × Aman Resorts. Locations
under consideration: Coorg, Mulshi, Alibaug. Luxury treehouses,
glass cabins, "Squash in the Wild" open-air courts. Programming:
breathwork + yoga + clinics + astronomy + farm-to-table.

### Vertical III — The "Squash Jailbreak" (Outdoor Courts)
India's first network of outdoor squash courts. 3 real-estate
models:
1. **Social Club pitch** — malls, rooftop bars, BKC/Gurgaon
   business parks. 62 sqm per court vs Padel's larger footprint.
2. **Adaptive Reuse** — defunct tennis courts, parking lots,
   dead mall atriums. Modular Access Courts install in days.
3. **PPP Leasing** — municipal parks, railway grounds, gymkhanas.
   15-yr operating lease; morning free/subsidized, evening
   commercial.

**6-month target (Oct 2026)**: 5 outdoor all-glass courts
established in the city.

### Vertical IV — The Outreach Program (Grassroots / Pathways)
Talent ID pipeline. **100% CSR-funded** (Tata / Reliance / JSW
fulfill Olympic-mandate CSR via this).
- **Phase 1**: mobile "Squash Vans" / inflatable courts in rural
  schools (HEC testing)
- **Phase 2**: regional hubs at PPP outdoor courts in Tier 2
  (Indore, Pune, Raipur)
- **Phase 3**: top 1% moved to Mumbai Flagship
- Philosophy: **"Scholar-Athletes"** → Ivy League scholarships
  (a meaningful parent draw)

## Competitive positioning

### vs squash clubs / academies
BAY is a **performance institution**, not a club or academy —
vertical ecosystem with Olympic-grade infrastructure (when
Excellence Centre opens), not a coaching schedule on rented courts.

### vs gyms / fitness centres / wellness brands
Wrong category. BAY is single-sport-focused; the wellness layer
(Sanctuary, Lab) is integrated infrastructure for athlete
performance, not a wellness offering.

### vs PSA-circuit individual coaches
Coaches teach. BAY makes introductions — matchplay, network,
sponsor exposure, scholarship pathways. The athlete handles the
game; BAY handles the institutional architecture around it.
