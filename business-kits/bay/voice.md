# Voice — BAY

Extracted from `BAY-Brand-Identity-System.html.pdf`,
[theblackandyellow.com](https://theblackandyellow.com), and
the recent BAY deck (Astra share #12).

**BAY operates two registers depending on audience.** Both are
authentic; the audience persona file picks which one applies.

| Register | When | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| **Liberating** | Public site, grassroots, athlete recruitment | Accessible, cultural, "breaking down barriers" |
| **Uncompromising** | Sponsors, investors, elite recruitment, Excellence track | Institutional, exclusive, "1% is forged" |

Both share the underlying institutional spine — direct, declarative,
specific.

---

## Tone in three words

*architectural · uncompromising · earned*

Both registers share these. "Liberating" tilts toward cultural-warmth;
"Uncompromising" tilts toward exclusivity. Neither is soft.

## How sentences look

- **Sentence length**: short and declarative for moments of force.
  BAY's signature is the punchy line that sits alone on a slide or
  page. Mixed for explanation.
- **Hedging**: never. BAY does not "believe" or "hope" anything.
- **Imperative voice**: occasional — used directly to athletes
  ("commit to the program", "show up").
- **Person**:
  - Third-person institutional ("BAY", "the program") for formal
    contexts (sponsor decks, press, partnership outreach)
  - Direct second-person ("you") for athlete-facing ("you handle
    the game")
  - First-person plural ("we") for partnership/sponsor docs ("we
    handle the introductions")
- **Active vs passive**: active. Passive is reserved for describing
  process neutrally.

## Words and phrases BAY DOES use

- **"Liberating Squash"** — the canonical brand verb
- **"performance institution"** — never "club", never "gym", never "academy"
- **"vertical ecosystem"** / **"under one standard"** — institutional framing
- **"Olympic ambition"** / **"national stage"** — competitive horizon
- **"glass court"** — the physical-infrastructure shorthand
- **"the 1% is forged"** — exclusivity register
- **"reset"** — for the Experience program (not "session", not "class")
- **"athletes"** — the people. Never "members", never "clients", never "customers"
- **"the introductions"** — what BAY does FOR athletes (matchplay, network, exposure)
- **"Black and Yellow"** spelled out, not "BAY" alone, in formal contexts
- **"earned access"** — for the meritocracy framing
- **"Double Dot"** — the visual identity device (gold dots)

## Words and phrases BAY NEVER uses

- **"squash club"** / **"sports club"** — wrong category
- **"fitness center"** / **"gym"** — wrong category
- **"members"** / **"clients"** / **"customers"** — wrong relationship
- **"wellness brand"** / **"lifestyle"** — wrong frame
- **"premium"** — describe the actual coaches/courts/standards instead
- **"boutique"** — too small a scale; BAY is institutional
- **"world-class"** / **"best-in-class"** — empty
- **"AI-powered"** — wrong company
- **"democratize"** — used in spirit but never as the verb (the
  liberating register uses "accessible" / "introductions" instead)

## Signatures and sign-offs

- **Email sign-off**:
  - `"— BAY"` for institutional comms
  - `"— Kunal Singh, Founder & Head Coach, Black And Yellow Squash"`
    when the founder credential matters (sponsorship, press, athlete
    recruitment for Excellence)
- **Doc footer**: `"© 2026 Black And Yellow Squash · CONFIDENTIAL · theblackandyellow.com"`
  (matches the existing deck's footer convention)
- **Slide deck closing**: the founder credential card —
  Kunal's contact + India Rank 18 / 2024 WUC Bronze /
  NDA Sports Blazer.

## Voice the audience should hear

**Layered.** BAY-as-organization speaks confidently, plurally, with
established authority — institutional voice for sponsors and press.
Founder-direct speaks personally, with specific competitive
credentials (India Rank 18, 2024 WUC Bronze) — for athlete
recruitment, partnership conversations, sponsor cold reach.

When generating sponsor decks (audience: `sponsor.md`), use
institutional voice. When generating outreach for elite-athlete
recruitment (audience: `athlete-recruit.md`), use founder voice.
When writing for grassroots / Pathways prospects, use the
Liberating register. The audience persona file says which.
