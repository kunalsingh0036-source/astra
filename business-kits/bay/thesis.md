# BAY thesis

## In one sentence

BAY is India's first vertical performance institution for squash —
Olympic-grade training infrastructure, clinical wellness, and cultural
prestige under one standard, currently operating from a Pilot Centre
at Badhwar Park, Colaba, with a 5-story flagship Excellence Centre as
the long-horizon build.

## Current state — the Pilot Centre at Badhwar Park

The Pilot Centre is BAY's operating reality. Four programs from the
website are running today; Street to Elite launches here next.

### Programs running today (per theblackandyellow.com)

1. **Experience** — The BAY Experience: a structured reset (squash,
   yoga, ice bath, breathwork) for adult professionals. Not a class.
   Not a session. A reset.
2. **Community** — Open-registration squash for adults who want
   competitive infrastructure without the elite-pathway intensity.
3. **Grassroots** — Introduction-to-squash and youth development.
   The funnel top.
4. **Excellence** — Elite / sub-elite athlete development. By
   application. Founder-coached. The pathway to Olympic ambition.

### Launching at the Pilot Centre

5. **Street to Elite** — the codified development pipeline
   connecting outdoor / community-court entry points to the
   Excellence track. Launches at the Pilot Centre as the bridge
   between the grassroots / community programs and the Excellence
   pathway.

### Yet to come (future Excellence Centre + Experience Centres)

The current pilot does not yet include — these come with the future
flagship build:

- **Experience Centres** — wellness-paradise model: Coorg / Mulshi /
  Alibaug. Luxury treehouses, glass cabins, "Squash in the Wild"
  open-air courts. Squash × Aman framing.
- **Nutrition Cafe** — Social Zone ground-floor of the future
  flagship. Public-facing, funnel top.
- **Membership tiers** — formal membership architecture for the
  flagship Excellence Centre.

## What BAY is

- **A performance institution**, not a club. Athletes, not customers.
- **Vertically integrated** — coaches, courts, conditioning, sports
  science, recovery — under one standard.
- **Olympic-aligned** — the path from Grassroots → Community →
  Street to Elite → Excellence leads to Asian Games and LA28
  qualification.
- **Founder-anchored** — Kunal Singh (India Rank 18, 2024 WUC Bronze,
  ex-Naval Officer) is the credible institutional face.

## The 4-vertical long-horizon vision

The Pilot Centre is the start. The full build-out (per the
partnership deck) is four verticals:

### Vertical I — The Excellence Centre (5-story flagship, Mumbai)
The institutional flagship. Benchmark: UFC Performance Institute ×
Reserve Padel.
- Ground floor: 2× outdoor glass courts ("Squash Porch") + Nutrition Café
- Level 1: 4× glass-back pro courts + 1× all-glass Gold show court
- Level 2: Elite S&C (VBT, Keiser, plyo zones)
- Level 3: VO2 Max, Force Plate, Motion Capture, physio + orthopedic partnerships
- Level 4: Cryotherapy, contrast baths, infrared saunas, hyperbaric O2

### Vertical II — BAY Experience Centres
Wellness-paradise model. Coorg / Mulshi / Alibaug. Treehouse / glass-
cabin residency with squash, breathwork, yoga, clinics, astronomy,
farm-to-table.

### Vertical III — Squash Jailbreak (outdoor courts)
India's first outdoor-squash network. 3 real-estate models: Social
Club pitch (malls, rooftops, BKC/Gurgaon parks), Adaptive Reuse
(defunct tennis courts, parking lots), PPP Leasing (municipal parks,
gymkhanas, railway grounds). 6-month target: 5 outdoor all-glass
courts established in the city.

### Vertical IV — Outreach Program (CSR-funded grassroots)
Talent ID pipeline. 100% CSR-funded (Tata / Reliance / JSW Olympic-
mandate fulfillment). Mobile Squash Vans → regional Tier-2 hubs →
top-1% migration to Mumbai flagship. "Scholar-Athletes" → Ivy League
scholarships.

## Why now

- **LA28 squash debut** — first Olympics for the sport
- **Asian Games Nagoya 2026 (Sep–Oct)** — the qualifier
- India has rising squash depth (Anahat Singh, Abhay Singh) but no
  unified performance infrastructure
- Mumbai has wealthy, status-conscious athletes seeking institutional-
  grade training, not generic fitness
- BAY's founder credentials — India Rank 18, 2024 WUC Bronze, ex-
  Naval Officer, RIMC alumnus — give the institution authority that
  athletes and sponsors both underwrite

## Forcing functions

- **April 2026 (now)**: Pilot Centre at Badhwar Park established
- **July 2026 (3 months)**: gravitate the city's entire squash
  attention to BAY; waitlists across all programs at the Pilot
  Centre
- **October 2026 (6 months)**: begin work on the future Excellence
  Centre; first 5 outdoor all-glass courts established (Vertical III);
  funding picked up
- **November 2026**: Kunal targets **National Champion 2026**
  (Nov 16–22). Founder-as-product — every tournament result feeds
  the institution's authority.

## What BAY is not

- Not a squash club
- Not a fitness centre
- Not a "wellness brand" / lifestyle aesthetic
- Not an academy targeting children alone — Grassroots serves them,
  but BAY's institutional gravity is the elite-track architecture
